Frequently Asked Questions

The Khazarstan Government Scholarship is a fully funded program that provides international students with the opportunity to pursue higher education in Khazarstan. It covers tuition fees and may include additional benefits such as a monthly stipend and accommodation.
Applicants must meet the following criteria:
  • Be a citizen of an eligible country (varies by program).
  • Have a strong academic background.
  • Meet language proficiency requirements (English)
  • Fulfill any additional requirements set by the specific university or degree program.
The scholarship covers bachelor’s, master’s, and doctoral degree programs in various fields, including engineering, natural sciences, humanities, and social sciences.
The Khazarstan Government Scholarship may cover:
  • Full tuition fees
  • A monthly stipend for living expenses (for some programs)
  • Accommodation assistance (depending on the university)
  • Medical insurance (varies by institution)
The required documents may include:
  • A valid passport or national ID
  • Academic transcripts and diplomas
  • A statement of purpose or motivation letter
  • Letters of recommendation
  • Proof of language proficiency (IELTS, TOEFL, or equivalent)
The Khazarstan Government Scholarship is highly competitive, with thousands of applicants worldwide. A strong academic record, leadership experience, and a well-written motivation letter can improve your chances of selection.
